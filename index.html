Nestled in the mist-kissed hills of Urumbikkara, Kerala, Nature Sanctuary is a 7000 sq ft wilderness retreat steeped in the British colonial legacy. Perched 2500 ft above sea level, Nature Sanctuary offers a serene escape with breathtaking views of valleys, waterfalls, and lush plantations of cardamom, pepper, vanilla, and tea.

This intimate hideaway offers a perfect blend of nature, luxury, and tranquility, making it an ideal destination for relaxation and adventure alike.
